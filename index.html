One million code to community

Dr. Richard M Stallman, commonly know as rms among the community, launched the free software movement in 1983 and started the development of the GNU operating system (see www.gnu.org) in 1984. GNU is free software: everyone has the freedom to copy it and redistribute it, with or without changes. It has been a revolution since then and gained strength day after day. The free software movement has changed the way software is looked upon. The growth of the free software movement is accredited to the contributions of many thousands of committed developers around the world.

MGU Kalathilakam from FISAT

Saranya Sasidharan,S8 CSE has won first prize in classical dance (Mohiniyattam) in the All India Inter University Youth Festival held at RTM University, Nagpur. At Nagpur she competed with the winners of zonal youth festivals that included Bharathanatyam, Kuchipudi, Manipuri, Kathak, Odissy and Assamese. Saranya had won first prizes in Mohiniyattam and Kuchipudi and second prize in Bharathanatyam in the M G University Youth Festival held at Kochi last year. She represented M G University at the zonal youth festival held at Chennai last month and won second prize in Mohiniyattam

Academic Heads

  • Dr. George Issac, B.Sc Engg., M.Tech, Ph.D
    Principal

    Dr. George Issac has taken charge as Principal of FISAT. He was the Principal of Mar Athanasius Engineering College Kothamangalam from June 2007 to May 2015. Dr. George Issac received B.Sc Engg (Mech.) degree from the University of Kerala in 1981. He took M.Tech in 1984 and Ph.D in 2003 from I I T Madras. He joined Kothamangalam Engineering College in 1982. In 2007, he was appointed Principal there. He was Expert Committee Member, Syllabus Revision Committee Member, Board of studies Member, in M G University during this time. He has published seven papers in international refereed journals. He is a Life member of Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE).

  • Dr. C Sheela, MSc, MPhil, CSIR(NET), Ph.D
    Vice-Principal

    Dr. C Sheela completed her M.Sc. in Mathematics from UC College, Aluva in 1988, and did her M.Phil. from Calicut University. She has her PhD in Fuzzy Topology from Cochin University. She has received CSIR-SRF Award. She has presented papers in many national/international conferences. In her 32 years of experience as teacher, she had guided many B.Tech projects. Dr. Sheela had worked as Principal-in-charge of IHRDE college. She is an active member of Kerala Mathematical Association and now serves as the regional secretary of the association. She is a life member of Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE).
